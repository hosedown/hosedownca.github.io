The 3-Step Refresh

01

We Arrive

We arrive on your scheduled trash day.

Just leave your bins out in the driveway and make sure we have access to water and electricity.

We’ll set up quickly and get ready to blast away the grime.

02

Deep Clean

Using high-pressure washing, we cut through all grime, residue, and leftover "mystery juice."

Our setup includes both a soap dispenser and a degreasing solution for maximum effectiveness.

The process takes about 15 minutes per bin.

03

Sanitize & Deodorize

Finally, we apply an eco-friendly deodorizer and natural cleaners, leaving your bins sanitized, odor-free, and fresh.

Your garage and curb will thank you! Quick, safe, and completely green.

Quick Questions

Do I need to be home?
Nope. Just leave your empty bins curbside on trash day.

Do you bring your own water and power?
We connect to your outdoor water spigot and a standard outlet to run our washer. It’s quick, safe, and uses less water than most garden hoses.

How long does it take?
About 15 minutes per bin. We wash, degrease, and sanitize inside and out.

Is it messy?
Not at all. We control runoff and leave your driveway cleaner than we found it.
