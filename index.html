Quick answers

Common Questions

Do I need to be home?

Nope. Just leave your empty bins curbside on trash day. We'll handle the rest and you'll come home to fresh, clean bins.

Do you bring your own water and power?

We connect to your outdoor spigot and a standard outlet. It's quick, safe, and uses less water than most garden hoses.

How long does the service take?

About 15 minutes per bin — we wash, degrease, and sanitize the inside and outside thoroughly.

Will it make a mess?

Not at all. We control all runoff and will leave your driveway cleaner than we found it.

What if it rains on my service day?

Light rain is no problem — we still clean. We'll reach out to reschedule if conditions are severe.

Are the cleaning products safe for kids and pets?

Yes. We use an EPA Safer Choice certified degreaser and finish with a plant-based probiotic deodorizer. No harsh residues, no synthetic fragrances — safe for kids, pets, and storm drains.
